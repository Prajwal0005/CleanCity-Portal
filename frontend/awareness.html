Waste Management Tips

Segregate Your Waste

Keep dry, wet, and hazardous waste separate for faster processing.

Use Biodegradable Bags

Choose eco-friendly bags to reduce plastic pollution in neighborhoods.

Compost Organic Waste

Turn kitchen waste into nutrient-rich compost for home gardens.

Reduce, Reuse, Recycle

Cut landfill load by reusing items and recycling regularly.

Proper E-Waste Disposal

Drop electronics at certified collection points to avoid toxins.

Support Local Clean-up Drives

Join community clean-ups to keep public spaces hygienic.
